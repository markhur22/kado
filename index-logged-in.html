    Frequently Asked Questions

    You can send a gift at our Gift Portal here. You’ll first be prompted to choose a gift from our curated Gift Emporium, before picking an e-card and writing them a message.

    You don’t need your recipient’s address in order to send the gift - you’ll receive a link which you can send to your lucky recipient, or we can send it on your behalf. Within this link, they’ll be able to see your message and e-card, confirm your gift (including picking any colours, sizes or types) or swap it for something of equal or lesser value. They’ll also confirm their address and shipping details.

    Your recipient is always able to swap their gift out for another gift of equal or lesser value. This means you have the best of both worlds - a thoughtful, hand-selected gift, with the flexibility for the recipient of swapping into our curated collection.

    You’ll only pay once your recipient has accepted your gift. We’ll email you a link, together with their thank you note.
